About Twix Chocolate

Twix Chocolate is a delicious chocolate bar made with a crunchy biscuit, caramel, and milk chocolate.

It was first introduced in the United Kingdom in 1967 and since then it has become a popular chocolate bar all around the world.

Products

  • Twix Original

    Twix Original is the classic Twix Chocolate bar that everyone loves.

  • Twix White

    Twix White is the same delicious Twix Chocolate bar with a white chocolate coating instead of milk chocolate.

  • Twix Peanut Butter

    Twix Peanut Butter is a twist on the classic Twix Chocolate bar with a peanut butter filling instead of caramel.
